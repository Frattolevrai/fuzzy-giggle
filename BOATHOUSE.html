Boathouse Facilities

The Centro Remiero's boathouse offers a variety of facilities to make your rowing experience enjoyable and convenient:

  • Boat storage and maintenance
  • Indoor and outdoor rowing machines
  • Locker rooms and showers
  • Cafeteria and lounge area

Our boathouse is open year-round and available to members and non-members. To learn more about membership, please visit our membership page.

Boat Rentals

We offer a variety of boats available for rent, including:

  • Single sculls
  • Double sculls
  • Quadruple sculls
  • Eights

To reserve a boat, please contact us at rentals@centroremiero.com.
