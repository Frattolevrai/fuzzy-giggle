Overview

The Centro Remiero's summer camp program offers children ages 8-14 a fun and engaging way to learn about rowing while enjoying the outdoors. Our experienced coaches will teach campers the basics of rowing and provide a variety of other activities to keep them active and entertained throughout the day.

The camp runs for eight weeks during the summer and is available to both members and non-members. We offer half-day and full-day options, as well as extended care for parents who need it.

Activities

  • Rowing instruction and practice
  • Swimming and water safety lessons
  • Outdoor games and sports
  • Arts and crafts
  • Nature walks and hikes

Registration

To register for the Centro Remiero summer camp, please complete our online registration form and submit payment. Spaces fill up quickly, so we recommend registering early to secure your spot. If you have any questions about the registration process or the camp itself, please contact us at summercamp@centroremiero.com.
