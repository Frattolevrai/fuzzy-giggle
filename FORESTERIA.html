Welcome to the Foresteria

The Foresteria at Centro Remiero is the perfect place to stay for rowing enthusiasts and anyone looking to enjoy the beauty of the lake region. Our charming rooms and comfortable amenities will make you feel right at home, and our convenient location puts you within easy reach of all the area has to offer.

Whether you're here for a rowing event or just to relax and unwind, we look forward to making your stay with us a memorable one.

Rooms and Suites

We offer a variety of room types to suit your needs, including single and double rooms and suites. All of our rooms are tastefully decorated and feature comfortable beds, private bathrooms, and scenic views of the lake or gardens.

Our suites are perfect for families or groups, with separate living areas and additional amenities like mini-fridges and coffee makers.

Amenities

  • Complimentary continental breakfast
  • Free Wi-Fi
  • Outdoor pool
  • On-site restaurant and bar
  • Fitness center

Rates

Our rates vary depending on room type, season, and length of stay. Please contact us for more information about rates and availability.
