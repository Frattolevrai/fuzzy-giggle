About the Challenge

The Rowing Winter Challenge is an annual event hosted by the Centro Remiero, open to all rowers and teams. The competition consists of a 5000-meter row on an indoor rowing machine, with the fastest times winning various prizes.

This year's event will take place on December 15th at 10am, and registration is open until December 1st. To register, please visit our registration page.
